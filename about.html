About me

and this space

Krunal Palande

that's me

Hello, my name is Krunal Palande. I live in a small town near Mumbai. I did my Bachelors in Commerce in 2005 and then worked in the Finance industry for about eight years. After getting bored with financial jargon, and frustrated with routine of calculating profits and losses, I decided to venture in a more dynamic field. I chose design but soon realised that I could use my data analysis skills that I learnt during my finance years, and so I started doing data visualisation. Later I taught myself user research, and UX/UI which helped me create innovative dashboards for my organisation. And ever since I've been designing data driven solutions, from static visualisations to interactive dataviz on BI tools. I created this space because I like telling stories with the help of data. These are my personal projects, about topics that interests me. Hope you like them.
